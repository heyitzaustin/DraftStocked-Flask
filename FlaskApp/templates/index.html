DraftStocked A simple and handy Draft Tool

    DraftStocked is a small but handy tool to aggregate information about Draft prospects in the 2015 NBA Draft. This tool combs through popular reddit threads from the past day/week/month to find relevant conversations about these prospects.

    Every comment concerning each player is put through a sentiment analysis engine to analyze whether the player has been spoken of positively or negatively lately. Each player than has a displayed 'Stock' and 'Change' to show how they've been doing/spoken of in the past few days!
